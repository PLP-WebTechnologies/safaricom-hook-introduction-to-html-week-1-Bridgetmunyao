Gender Based Violence

The Kenyan government recently declared gender-based violence as a top threat since 100 women are killed in 4 months, mostly by known perprtrators. In response, a specialized police unit was formed to address femicides, while human rights groups organized protests seeking justice


Legal proceedings

Edwin Chiloba's murder convition: Jacktone Odhiambo has been sentenced to 50 years in prison for the murder of LGBTQ+ activist Edwin Chiloba. Chiloba's mutilated body was discovered in a metal box nearly 2 years ago, drawing international attention to LGBTQ rights in Kenya


Energy sector

Kenya has extended its oil supply deal with Gulf firms, allowing 180-day credit terms for petroleum imports. This reduces dollar demand, stabilizes the shilling, and lowers fuel prices. The cabinet hasn't disclosed the extension's duration
